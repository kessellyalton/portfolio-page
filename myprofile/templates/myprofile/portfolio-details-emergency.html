This is an example of portfolio detail

I led Liberia's Education Sector Response to COVID-19, implementing policy priorities, strategies, and activities to ensure student safety, care, and continuous learning. This comprehensive plan included access to nutritional, psycho-social, health, and well-being support, as well as safe school re-openings.

Our interventions facilitated school reopening, recovery, and system strengthening to provide quality education for all. The plan ensured fairness and equitable service provision across locations, genders, and socio-economic statuses. It emphasized learning continuity for all students, including those with special needs and girls, and targeted learners at all levels of the education system through a system-wide approach.
