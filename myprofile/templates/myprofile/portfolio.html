Portfolio

My portfolio highlights a decade of leadership in educational development, research, and data analysis, marked by significant achievements and innovations. I have successfully led multi-stakeholder initiatives resulting in a crucial compact with the Global Partnership for Education and secured over USD 300 million in direct donor support for educational projects. My oversight extended to all Ministry of Education projects, including those worth over USD 200 million supported by development partners. I spearheaded the creation of Liberia's new Education Sector Plan and conducted comprehensive sector analyses.
Additionally, I established national School Quality Standards and a literacy and numeracy assessment system for key grades. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I directed Liberia's education al response, launched the dormant Liberia Institute for Science and Technology, and developed strategies for STEM prioritization and disaster risk management in education.

In data analysis, I led the development and digitization of the Ministry of Education's data collection tools, managing the Annual School Censuses from 2019 to 2022.
My research spans issues at the frontier of human knowledge, including black holes and dark energy. These research, data analysis, and app development skills, coupled with a deep understanding of Deep Learning algorithms, can be effectively applied to solving everyday problems.
